Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data We Collect

LinkWords stores game progress (scores, streaks, achievements) locally on your device using localStorage. If you sign in, your progress is synced to Firebase Firestore under your authenticated account.

Authentication

We use Firebase Authentication (Google, Apple, Facebook sign-in). We store only your display name, email, and profile photo URL for account display purposes.

Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services. No personal data is shared with advertisers.

Data Storage

All game data is stored either locally on your device or in Google Firebase (Firestore) if you choose to sign in. Data is encrypted in transit via HTTPS.

Your Rights

You can export your data from Settings. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Account section in Settings.
