Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

What We Collect

LinkWords collects minimal data to provide the game experience:

  • Game progress — scores, streaks, achievements, and settings are stored locally on your device using localStorage.
  • Account data — if you sign in (optional), your display name, email, and profile photo from your authentication provider (Google, Apple, or Facebook) are used for cloud sync and leaderboard display.
  • Analytics — with your consent, we use Firebase Analytics to understand usage patterns. No personal data is shared with third parties beyond Google Analytics.

Cloud Sync

If you create an account, your game data is synced to Google Cloud Firestore. This data is accessible only to your authenticated account and is protected by Firestore security rules.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use localStorage (not cookies) to save game state, preferences, and consent choices. No third-party tracking cookies are set by LinkWords itself.

Advertising

The mobile app may show ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization unless you opt out in your device settings. You can remove ads with an in-app purchase.

Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You can:

  • Export your data via Settings > Account
  • Delete your account and all associated cloud data via Settings > Account > Delete Account
  • Withdraw analytics consent at any time via the GDPR banner or Settings

Data Retention

Cloud data is retained until you delete your account. Local data is retained until you clear your browser data or uninstall the app.
